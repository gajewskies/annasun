ABOUT ANNASUN

Started in 2012 by Erik Corrie (vocals) as a solo project, Annasun has evolved in many ways. Beginning with the intent of staying an acoustic project, Corrie asked vocalist and guitarist, Bryan Williams (rhythm guitar, vocals) to join the project. Corrie and Williams found they had great chemistry in both songwriting and performing, which lead them to playing as a acoustic duo on many venues and around the New York area. While attending the SUNY college at Fredonia in 2013- 2014, Corrie and Williams had written enough material to begin playing larger shows, even landing themselves an opportunity in 2014 to share the stage with the internationally know, Walk The Moon. As the need for more member became apparent, the duo expanded their sound, with the addition of Cody Tarbox (bass), Ryan Schindler (lead Guitar), Brian McMahon (percussion). Annasun creates a live acoustic performance that features a large variety of vocalists and instruments with an Indie Folk sound.
